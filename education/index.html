PRINCIPAL’S MESSAGE

Welcome to RMPS

Children are the dream of their parents. Every Father wants to make his child a good citizen and a good human being. I am sure that Raj Modern Public School is a best platform where parents aim will be fulfilled.

RMPS awares the children with scholastic abilities and life skills which are essential for success. We believe that RMPS is a wonderful school where youth and experience would come together to explore the purpose of learning about life and living.

We do encourage your children to explore, learn and realize their potential as a scholar, a leader, a positive thinker, a social worker and an ideal person. We believe that the students of RMPS will be ready to step into global society with honor. In this small town, the students of Raj Modern Public school feel proud of their school which makes them proud of their school which makes more competitive and confident than big cities.

Raj Modern Public school [RMPS] encourages each student to have focus in life- a vision accompanied with idealism. To use their knowledge to strengthen and re-establish moral values which are fast depleting in our society.

The total strength of the school is approximately 1200 students. We take immense pride in knowing each and every individual child’s strength and weakness and help them create a sense of responsibility and independence along with value based quality education.

Mrs. Uma Balasubramanium

Principal

CURRICULUM

As an English medium school, Raj Modern Public School is recognized by the Directorate of Education, Government of National Capital Territory of Delhi.
RMPS is affiliated to the Central Board of Secondary Education, Delhi for All India Secondary and Senior Secondary Examinations.
The emphasis is on language, numerical and aesthetic development along with Nature Study through activity and play that is conductive to the physical and emotional growth of the children in their respective age groups.

At the senior secondary level, the school provides following cources:
Commerce: English core, Business studies, Accountancy, Economics and Maths / Physical education.
Humanities: English core, History, Political Science, Economics/Hindi and Physical Education

About RMPS

This school was established by the Raje Ram Education society in 2003. It is a co-educational, unaided English Medium School, It is recognised by the Directorate of Education, Delhi and is affiliated to the Central Board of Secondary Education [CBSE].
