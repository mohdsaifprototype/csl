OUR VISION

Raj Modern Public School inculcates important traits like Obedience, Sincerity and Loyalty among both students and teachers. The school provides the necessary means and requisite ambience so that the students gain a cosmopolitan outlook of life.

The guiding principal of the school has been based on our motto “Work is Worship”. We always strive to impact complete education an education that works and is beyond academics.

We focus on multi-dimentional growth of our pupils by helping them build on necessary personality traits like confidence, knowledge, behavior, intelligence, communication, analytical abilities, decision making, team building, aim setting etc.

About RMPS

This school was established by the Raje Ram Education society in 2003. It is a co-educational, unaided English Medium School, It is recognised by the Directorate of Education, Delhi and is affiliated to the Central Board of Secondary Education [CBSE].
The school offers a wide range of cources at the +2 level in two streams, namely Commerce and Humanities. All the classes are taught about computers so that they are able to keep in step with the times.
The school not only aims at the academic growth of its students but also strives to achieve excellence in other aspects of their personality. Emphasis is laid on imparting moral values and providing the right environment for the all – round development of their personality and self-esteem.
Inculcation of values as well as innovative and progressive methods in the teaching- learning process have helped the school establish a reputation for excellence and carve out a niche for itself among the leading schools in Delhi.
At Raj Modern School, we are passionate about the importance of providing each student with a complete sense of belonging, of optimism and success, of being unique and of being heard. Our greatest responsibility is to assist our students in building personal values, spirit, knowledge and vision.
