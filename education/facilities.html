RMPS Facilites

Raj Modern Public School has a large building complex with all modern amenities. The effort is to develop a multi-dimensional, inter- disciplinary paradigm of teaching. The School provides for the all round development of students.

Games and Sports

The School provides facilities for all games and sports. The students who want to make their career in sports CP Gurukul offers a competitive sports program through CP Gurukul Sports Academy.

Yoga and Health

Yoga lessons form a part of the school curriculum and are used in training the mind and body for self- discipline. The school provides first aid at the school premises.

Library

Our School library provides academic resources for all constituents of the school students, faculty and administrative staff. The library strives to make resources readily accessible when and wherever they are needed to enhance and promote the total growth and development of the students.

Smart Classes

Comprehensive Laboratory System

To enhance learning ability and skill and impact practical exposure about theoretical concepts, the school has a large number of modern laboratory systems.

About RMPS

This school was established by the Raje Ram Education society in 2003. It is a co-educational, unaided English Medium School, It is recognised by the Directorate of Education, Delhi and is affiliated to the Central Board of Secondary Education [CBSE].
